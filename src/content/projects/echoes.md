I have lost an unreasonable number of hours to incremental games, so the
obvious next step was to lose even more hours making one. Echoes is my own
take on the genre — numbers that go up, systems that unfold, and all the
surprisingly hard design questions hiding underneath something that looks
simple.

It's a sandbox for learning game loops, balance, and the discipline of
shipping something playable rather than endlessly polishing something perfect.

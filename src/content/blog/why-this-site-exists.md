---
title: Why this site exists
date: 2026-07-16
summary: Years of running services on this domain and never once having a front page.
---

I've owned gazaka.com for years. It runs a handful of services I've built and
hosted myself, and in all that time the root of the domain has served exactly
nothing. If you visited gazaka.com you got a 404.

So this is the fix. A front page for the things I've made, and somewhere to
write the occasional note when something has been rattling around my head
long enough to be worth writing down.

About the writing: there's no schedule and no plan. Some posts will be about
servers and databases and debugging things at midnight. Some might be about
homebrewing or games. It's a notebook that happens to be public.

If you're here because I sent you the link, hello, this is what I do for fun.
If you found it some other way, I'm genuinely curious how.

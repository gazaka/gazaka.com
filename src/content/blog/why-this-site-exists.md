---
title: Why this site exists
date: 2026-07-16
summary: Years of running services on this domain and never once having a front door. Time to fix that.
---

I've owned gazaka.com for years. It runs a small collection of services I've
built and hosted myself, and in all that time it has never had an actual
landing page. If you visited the root of the domain, you got nothing. The
cobbler's children have no shoes; the homelabber's domain has no homepage.

So this is it — the front door. A place to put the things I've made, the
things I've learned, and the occasional brain dump when something has been
rattling around my head long enough to be worth writing down.

Fair warning about the writing: there's no schedule, no niche, and no
newsletter. This is a notebook that happens to be public. Some posts will be
about servers and databases and the specific flavour of confusion that comes
from debugging something at midnight. Some might be about homebrewing or
games. We'll see.

If you're here because I sent you the link — hello, this is what I do for fun.
If you found it some other way — genuinely, how?
